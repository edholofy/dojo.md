KEY STATISTICS AND BENCHMARKS INCLUDED
================================================================================

RESPONSE TIME TARGETS (Industry Standard):
  - P1/Critical: 15-30 minutes response
  - P2/High:     1-4 hours response
  - P3/Medium:   8-24 hours response
  - P4/Low:      48+ hours response

UPTIME SLA TARGETS:
  - Standard:            99% (87.6 min downtime/month)
  - Enterprise:          99.9% (43.2 min downtime/month)
  - Premium:             99.99% (4.3 min downtime/month)
  - Ultra-high-reliability: 99.999% (26 sec downtime/month)

SERVICE CREDITS:
  - Monthly cap:  50% of service fees
  - Annual cap:   4 months of fees
  - Range:        5-25% per breach
  - Google SRE postmortem: 48 hours

DETECTION AND MONITORING:
  - Detection target:  < 15 minutes
  - Notification time: < 30 minutes
  - Modern improvement: 80% faster detection
  - Reduction in disputes: 50%

BREACH STATISTICS (2025-2026):
  - US incidents: 3,332 (79% increase over 5 years)
  - Average cost: USD 5.47 million
  - Detection time: 181 days average
  - Containment: 60 days average
  - Cyberattacks: 80% of breaches
  - Human error: 40% of breaches
  - Insider threats: 22% of breaches

CUSTOMER IMPACT:
  - Single 5-hour outage: 5-8% churn risk
  - Repeated breaches: 15-25% churn
  - Multiple in 6 months: 40%+ churn
  - No communication: 2x churn vs proactive

ESCALATION MATRIX:
  P1 (Critical):
    0 min    → Page on-call
    10 min   → Manager escalation
    15 min   → War room creation
    30 min   → VP escalation
  
  P2 (High):
    30-45 min → Manager notification if unresolved

GOVERNANCE:
  - Quarterly SLA reviews: Standard
  - Annual revisions: Mandatory
  - Fast-growing companies: 2-3x annual reviews
  - Contract lifecycle: Monthly reconciliation, quarterly reviews
