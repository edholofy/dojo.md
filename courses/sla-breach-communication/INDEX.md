### Key Statistics Quick Reference

#### Response Time Targets
- P1/Critical: 15-30 minutes
- P2/High: 1-4 hours
- P3/Medium: 8-24 hours
- P4/Low: 48+ hours

#### Uptime SLA Targets
- Standard: 99% (87.6 min/month downtime)
- Enterprise: 99.9% (43.2 min/month downtime)
- Premium: 99.99% (4.3 min/month downtime)
- Ultra-high-reliability: 99.999% (26 sec/month downtime)

#### Service Credit Caps
- Monthly maximum: 50% of service fees
- Annual maximum: 4 months of service fees
- Typical credit range: 5-25% per breach

#### Breach Statistics (2025-2026)
- US incidents: 3,332 (79% increase over 5 years)
- Average cost: USD 5.47 million
- Detection time: 181 days average
- Containment time: 60 days average
- Churn risk (single 5-hour outage): 5-8%
- Churn risk (multiple breaches): 40%+

#### Governance
- Quarterly SLA reviews: Standard
- Annual SLA revisions: Mandatory
- Fast-growing: 2-3x annual reviews
- Contract lifecycle: Monthly reconciliation, quarterly reviews
